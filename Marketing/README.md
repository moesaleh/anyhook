# AnyHook — Marketing Materials

This folder contains the executive, product, and marketing collateral for **AnyHook**, the real-time subscription-proxy platform that connects GraphQL and WebSocket data sources to webhook endpoints.

## Contents

| # | Document | Audience | Purpose |
|---|----------|----------|---------|
| 1 | [Executive_Summary.md](./Executive_Summary.md) | Executives, investors, board | One-page strategic overview of the product, market, and traction |
| 2 | [Product_Overview.md](./Product_Overview.md) | Product managers, evaluators | Detailed feature map across all 15 product areas |
| 3 | [Feature_Highlights.md](./Feature_Highlights.md) | Engineers, technical buyers | Capability-by-capability breakdown with proof points |
| 4 | [Use_Cases.md](./Use_Cases.md) | Sales, solutions engineers | Concrete industry scenarios and ROI stories |
| 5 | [Competitive_Positioning.md](./Competitive_Positioning.md) | Sales, marketing | Where AnyHook wins vs. build-it-yourself and SaaS competitors |
| 6 | [One_Pager.md](./One_Pager.md) | Conferences, leave-behinds | Printable single-page summary |
| 7 | [Launch_Announcement.md](./Launch_Announcement.md) | Press, social, blog | Press-release-style launch copy |
| 8 | [Email_Campaign.md](./Email_Campaign.md) | Demand gen | Drip sequence + cold outbound templates |

| 10 | [FAQ.md](./FAQ.md) | Everyone | Common questions from prospects and customers |

## How to use this collateral

- **Quick pitch in a meeting?** Open the [One_Pager](./One_Pager.md).
- **Email a CTO?** Lead with the [Executive Summary](./Executive_Summary.md) and attach the [Technical Brief](./Technical_Brief.md).
- **Booth conversation?** Walk through [Use Cases](./Use_Cases.md) and hand over the [One_Pager](./One_Pager.md).
- **Blog post or launch?** Adapt [Launch_Announcement.md](./Launch_Announcement.md).
- **RFP response?** Draw from [Feature_Highlights](./Feature_Highlights.md) + [Technical Brief](./Technical_Brief.md).

## Source-of-truth

All content here is derived from the canonical repository documentation:

- `README.md` — public project overview
- `productfeatures.md` — feature tracker (status: DONE / PARTIAL / TODO)
- `docs/openapi.yaml` — API surface
- `docs/RUNBOOK.md` — operational posture
- `docker-compose.yml` — deployment topology

When the product changes, update the source documents first, then refresh the collateral here.
